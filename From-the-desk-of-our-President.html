      Radio Rishikesh FM 90.0 MHz. Community Radio Station.

      Our community radio preserves local culture and traditions and provides high-quality, informative, and entertaining content to listeners. Community radio provides an open mic for people from communities who are often overlooked by other broadcast media. This is because we don’t chase the big audiences, instead we look for an audience made up of the people of the community. Radio Rishikesh FM 90.0 MHz community radio emphasises direct communication with the audience and puts community issues first & opportunity to express their diverse interests, concerns, and points of view..

"We empower underprivileged rural women by mobilizing them into self-help groups, providing skill development training, and encouraging self-employment opportunities. Through cooperatives, clusters, and producer groups, we strengthen both farm and non-farm artisans. By offering raw materials, capacity building, value chain development, and marketing support, we ensure sustainable livelihoods and financial inclusion for women and their communities."

Bhartiya Gramotthan Sanstha is dedicated to providing sustainable livelihood opportunities and an improved quality of life to underprivileged families. We focus on addressing critical issues such as health, education, water, agriculture, and environment.

Dear members of our esteemed organisation,

I hope this message finds you all in good health and high spirits. Today, I would like to address a crucial matter that I believe holds great potential for our organisation and our broader goals of sustainable development. Handicrafts are not just beautiful pieces of art, but they also represent the rich cultural heritage and traditions of our beloved nation. By promoting the production and sale of handcrafted products, we can reduce our reliance on mass-produced goods that often exploit natural resources and have negative environmental consequences. Handicrafts are typically made from sustainable materials, using traditional techniques that have been passed down through generations, making them much more eco-friendly. By emphasizing handicraft development, we can contribute to reducing our carbon footprint and fostering a more environmentally conscious society.

I urge all members of our organisation to come together and actively participate in this endeavor. Let us not forget our responsibility towards sustainable development and preserving our cultural heritage. By placing handicraft development at the forefront of our efforts, we can create a positive impact on our economy, environment, and society as a whole. Together, we can bring about a future where handicrafts thrive, communities prosper, and sustainable development becomes a reality.

Mrs. Gita Chandola

President
Bhartiya Gramotthan Sanstha



Our efforts and achievements stand as a testament to our continuous commitment to sustainable impact.”
