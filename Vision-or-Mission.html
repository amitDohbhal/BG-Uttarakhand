      Radio Rishikesh FM 90.0 MHz. Community Radio Station.

      Our community radio preserves local culture and traditions and provides high-quality, informative, and entertaining content to listeners. Community radio provides an open mic for people from communities who are often overlooked by other broadcast media. This is because we don’t chase the big audiences, instead we look for an audience made up of the people of the community. Radio Rishikesh FM 90.0 MHz community radio emphasises direct communication with the audience and puts community issues first & opportunity to express their diverse interests, concerns, and points of view..

Strengthening Women, Strengthening Society, Dignity Through Development

36 years of excellence

“At the heart of our work is a simple belief – every family deserves dignity. We support underprivileged communities through education, health, and income generation, placing women at the center of change. By empowering women, we uplift families and build stronger, healthier societies—creating lasting change for a better future.”

  • Vision of the Organisation

    1: “To build a just and inclusive society where underprivileged families, especially women, have access to education, health, and sustainable livelihoods, enabling them to live with dignity and self-reliance.”
    2: “To create empowered communities where every woman and family can thrive through knowledge, wellness, and economic independence.”
    3: “To be a catalyst for social change by improving the quality of life of underprivileged families and fostering women-led development.”
    4: “A future where equality, opportunity, and empowerment transform lives and strengthen communities.”

  • Mission of the Organisation

    1. “To enhance the lives of underprivileged families by promoting education, health & hygiene, and sustainable livelihoods, with a strong focus on women’s empowerment and capacity building.”
    2. “To implement government and non-government programs that improve access to opportunities, strengthen communities, and enable families to achieve self-reliance.”
    3. “To work hand-in-hand with communities in creating pathways for growth, dignity, and empowerment—ensuring that women lead the way in shaping a better tomorrow.”

Several projects were organized to promote the diversification of our natural products, creating new opportunities for innovation and income generation. This initiative not only provided employment to local artisans but also aimed at offering the rural communities of Uttarakhand a sustainable and reliable livelihood option, thereby contributing to both economic empowerment and eco-friendly development.
