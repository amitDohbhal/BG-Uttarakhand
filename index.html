From The Desk Of President

Dear members of our esteemed organisation, I hope this message finds you all in good health and high spirits. Today, I would like to address a crucial matter that I believe holds great potential for our organisation and our broader goals of sustainable development.


Handicrafts are not just beautiful pieces of art, but they also represent the rich cultural heritage and traditions of our beloved nation. By promoting the production and sale of handcrafted products, we can reduce our reliance on mass-produced goods that often exploit natural resources and have negative environmental consequences. Handicrafts are typically made from sustainable materials, using traditional techniques that have been passed down through generations, making them much more eco-friendly. By emphasizing handicraft development, we can contribute to reducing our carbon footprint and fostering a more environmentally conscious society.

I urge all members of our organisation to come together and actively participate in this endeavor. Let us not forget our responsibility towards sustainable development and preserving our cultural heritage. By placing handicraft development at the forefront of our efforts, we can create a positive impact on our economy, environment, and society as a whole. Together, we can bring about a future where handicrafts thrive, communities prosper, and sustainable development becomes a reality.

Mrs. Gita Chandola

President

देहरादून: भारतीय ग्रामोत्थान संस्था (BGS) को हाल ही में एस.डी.जी अचीवर अवार्ड से सम्मानित किया गया, जो समाज के हर वर्ग के लिए प्रेरणा का स्रोत बन गया है। यह अवार्ड संस्था के महिलाओं के सशक्तिकरण, रोजगार सृजन, और ग्रामीण विकास में किए गए अद्वितीय योगदान को मान्यता देता है। इस सम्मान के साथ ही संस्था ने साबित कर दिया कि समाज में स्थायित्व और समृद्धि लाने के लिए केवल विकास योजनाओं की आवश्यकता नहीं, बल्कि महिला सशक्तिकरण की दिशा में किए गए कार्य भी अत्यंत महत्वपूर्ण हैं।

श्री मुख्यमंत्री पुष्कर सिंह धामी द्वारा सोमवार को मुख्यमंत्री आवास स्थित मुख्य सेवक सदन में आयोजित एस.डी.जी एचीवर अवार्ड समारोह में भारतीय ग्रामोत्थान संस्था सहित नौ अन्य संस्थानों, चार औद्योगिक प्रतिष्ठानों और तीन व्यक्तियों को सम्मानित किया गया। इस समारोह में गगन त्रिपाठी, गुरजीत सिंह और सुबोध शाह जैसे प्रमुख व्यक्तियों को भी पुरस्कार मिला।

“From Skills to Strength – Building Futures for Women”

“Empowering Hands, Transforming Lives”

36+ Years Of Experience

Bhartiya Gramotthan Sanstha is dedicated to driving holistic community development by empowering women and uplifting underprivileged families. Through skill-based handicraft training using local resources, the organization equips women with income-generating capabilities while fostering Self-Help Groups (SHGs), enhancing financial literacy, and linking beneficiaries with government schemes, housing support, loans, and entrepreneurial opportunities. The creation of the Rishikesh Production Company provides a sustainable platform for women artisans to market their products, paving the way for financial independence and long-term livelihood security.

In parallel, the Sanstha places a strong emphasis on education for underprivileged children, offering scholarships, after-school coaching, computer training, and access to library facilities. Special focus is directed towards marginalized communities through initiatives such as the Van Gujjar School, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to learn, grow, and build a brighter future.

Through skill training and innovation.

By the joint efforts of BGS and the Jute Service Centre, Rishikesh, Natural fiber was introduced as a sustainable raw material in the region. Artisans and women Self-Help Groups (SHGs) received both basic and technical training in raw fiber processing and product development.

Several projects were organized to promote the diversification of our natural products, creating new opportunities for innovation and income generation. This initiative not only provided employment to local artisans but also aimed at offering the rural communities of Uttarakhand a sustainable and reliable livelihood option, thereby contributing to both economic empowerment and eco-friendly development.
